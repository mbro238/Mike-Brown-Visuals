My Eyes, Stories Lie.

My entire life I had aspirations of always being in the picture. This is until I picked up a camera my Junior year of high school. My eyes were opened once I took my first picture. I felt like an artist, as I discoverd that something as simple as changing an angle could change the entire meaning of a picture. Being a kid from a small town in Mississippi was not easy. I caught on to a lot things at a young age. For instance, I noticed that a lot of people are trained/ brain washed to think the same way. This is why a lot of individuals are scared to go out and beyond expectations to be different. Little do many know that the opposite of conformity is not cowardliness, but it is conformity. Everyone is just copying the ones around them and they don't know why or realize where they are going. That's why I, Micheal Brown, took the initiative to step outside of my comfort zone & go somewhere no else has been. One of my biggest aspirations is to become the closest thing to hope for a small town in Mississippi.
